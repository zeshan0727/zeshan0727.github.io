Concept A development for Apple Messages
v0.2.1 Diagnostic

Purpose of this release

This diagnostic build identifies the exact Messages controllers, cells, transcript views, bubble views and composer classes used by iOS 16.0 on the physical device. The report will be used to produce the real Concept A interface in v0.3.0.

Privacy: message text, contact names, phone numbers and participant information are not collected.

How to test

1. Open the Messages inbox.
2. Open one conversation, then return to the inbox.
3. Open Settings → Next Message.
4. Tap Share Last Report and send the TXT file back in the ChatGPT conversation.

Compatibility

iOS 15 and later • RootHide and rootless • arm64 and arm64e

Primary test device: iPhone 14 Pro Max, iOS 16.0, RootHide.
